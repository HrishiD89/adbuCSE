Facilities/ Equipment Available:

  • State-of-the-art Infrastructure: Our CSE labs are equipped with modern and advanced infrastructure to facilitate cutting-edge research and practical learning.
  • High-Performance Computers: Students have access to high-performance computers with the latest processors and sufficient memory for resource-intensive tasks.
  • Specialized Software: The labs are equipped with licensed software tools for programming, data analysis, simulation, and other specialized applications.
  • Internet Connectivity: Reliable and high-speed internet connectivity ensures seamless access to online resources and collaboration with peers worldwide.
  • Testing and Debugging Tools: Students can utilize various testing and debugging tools to ensure the accuracy and efficiency of their software projects.
  • Research Equipment: Labs are equipped with research-grade equipment for conducting experiments in areas like artificial intelligence, networking, and IoT.
