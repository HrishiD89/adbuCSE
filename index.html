About Our Department

The CSE department at Assam Don Bosco University aims to provide quality education in the field of computer science and engineering. It offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in computer science, focusing on both theoretical and practical aspects of the discipline. The department also offers research opportunities for students interested in pursuing a Ph.D. in computer science.

The curriculum of the CSE department is likely to cover various subjects such as programming languages, data structures, algorithms, computer networks, database management systems, software engineering, artificial intelligence, and more.
